Maharashtra Specialties

  • Delicious Cuisine: Vada Pav, Pav Bhaji, Puran Poli, Misal Pav

  • Religious Significance: Siddhivinayak Temple, Shirdi Sai Baba Temple
  • Festivals: Ganesh Chaturthi, Gudi Padwa, Makar Sankranti
